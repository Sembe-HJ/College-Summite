About The Summit

Empowering Innovation,Shaping the Future

KIUTech Summit is the premier anuual technology conference organized by the Tech community at Kampala International University in Tanzania(KIUT). We bring together the brightest young minds,industry experts, and Tech enthuasists to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world innovations.

Our Missions

Mission is to foster a vibrant ecosystem where students can showcase their technical progress, learn from industry leaders and collaborate on solutions that address local and global challenges through technology.

Why Attend?

Wheather U a seasoned coder, a budding designer, or curious beginner, KIUTech Summit offers a platform to:

    • Level Up: Gain practical skills that aren't always teach on Classrooms.
      Showcase: Present your projects to a wide audience and get valuable feedback.
      Connect: Join a community of innovators who are passionate about the future of ICT in Africa
